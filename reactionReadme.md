# 🎮 Reaction Tester Game

A fast-paced and fun reaction speed game that challenges your reflexes!

## 🕹️ How It Works

- The game displays a **3x3 grid (9 tiles)**.
- A color of your choice will **flash randomly** in one of the grid tiles.
- Your goal is to **click the flashing color before it disappears**.
- Each successful click earns you points.
- The faster your reaction, the better your score!

## 🏆 Scoring System

- ✅ Each successful click = **10 points**
- ❌ Missed clicks = No points
- The score counter keeps track of your total points.

## 🎯 Difficulty Levels

Choose your challenge:

- 🟢 **Easy**
  - Slower flash speed
  - Color stays visible longer
  - Great for beginners

- 🟡 **Medium**
  - Faster flashing
  - Color disappears quicker
  - More challenging

- 🔴 **Hard**
  - Very fast flashing
  - Very short visibility time
  - Ultimate reaction test!

- Simple and fun gameplay
